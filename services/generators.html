Standby Generator Installation in Northern Arkansas

Power outages in southwest Arkansas can happen any time — from severe thunderstorms and ice storms to utility failures. A properly installed standby generator keeps your home or business running through it all. Bow Tie Solutions handles the complete electrical side of generator installation, from sizing your system and installing the transfer switch to wiring into your electrical panel.

We work with leading generator brands and can handle any size installation — from a portable generator with a manual transfer switch to a whole-home automatic standby system that kicks on within seconds of a power failure. We coordinate with your gas company and ensure all work meets local code and manufacturer requirements.

  • Whole-home standby generator installation
  • Automatic transfer switch (ATS) installation
  • Manual transfer switch installation
  • Generator circuit and panel connection
  • Portable generator interlock kit installation
  • Commercial backup power systems
  • Critical load panel installation

Generator Installation FAQ

Generator sizing depends on what you want to power during an outage. A 10-16kW unit typically covers essential circuits like lights, refrigerator, HVAC, and sump pump. A whole-home generator of 20kW+ can power everything simultaneously. We'll calculate your load and recommend the right size during your free estimate.

The transfer switch safely isolates your generator from the utility line, protecting utility workers and your equipment. Never run a generator without a proper transfer switch.
