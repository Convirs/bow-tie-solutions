Industrial Electrical Services

Heavy-duty electrical for manufacturing plants, warehouses, and industrial facilities across southwest Arkansas.

Industrial Electricians in Northern Arkansas

Industrial electrical systems demand a higher level of expertise, precision, and safety. Bow Tie Solutions's team is experienced in all aspects of industrial electrical — from three-phase power distribution and motor control centers to machine wiring, conduit systems, and facility-wide electrical upgrades. We've worked with manufacturers and industrial facilities throughout southwest Arkansas for over two decades.

When your production line is down, every minute is money. We understand the urgency and are equipped to troubleshoot, repair, and upgrade industrial electrical systems efficiently and safely. We also work proactively with plant managers and maintenance teams to schedule work during planned downtime.

  • Three-phase power installation and upgrades
  • Motor controls and motor control centers (MCCs)
  • Industrial machine and equipment wiring
  • Conduit installation (EMT, rigid, PVC)
  • Industrial panel and switchgear installation
  • Electrical system troubleshooting and diagnostics
  • Warehouse and facility lighting systems
