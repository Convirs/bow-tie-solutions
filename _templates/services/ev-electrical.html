EV Charger Installation FAQ

Level 1 charging uses a standard 120V outlet and adds roughly 3-5 miles of range per hour — suitable for plug-in hybrids or overnight charging when you drive fewer miles. Level 2 uses a dedicated 240V circuit and adds 20-30+ miles per hour, making it the recommended choice for most EV owners who drive regularly.

It depends on your current panel's available capacity. We assess your panel during the estimate visit. Many homes have enough capacity to add a 240V EV circuit without a full panel upgrade. If your panel is at capacity, we'll give you an honest recommendation on what's needed.
